## The 2-Second Hook Framework for Brand Stories

**12 Essential Brand Story Hooks:**

1. Provocative Question Hook - Opens with direct question that challenges assumptions
2. Founder's Hands at Work Hook - Close-up of hands building/creating
3. Product-in-World Establishing Shot - Product appears naturally in someone's life
4. Emotional Human Moment Hook - Gesture of connection in first 1-2 seconds
5. Surprising Statistic or Contradiction Hook - Stark visual contrast
6. Time-Lapse Transformation Hook - Seed to plant, blank canvas to masterpiece
7. Unexpected Connection Hook - Two unrelated moments cut together reveal truth
8. The Problem Hook - Open on friction, pain, or unmet need
9. Cultural Moment Hook - Brand at intersection of meaning or celebration
10. Sensory Hook - Begin with texture, sound, tactile experience
11. Silhouette or Shadow Hook - Backlit figure creating mystery
12. Continuity Hook - Open with video already in motion

## Brand Storytelling Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
- Narrative Arc: Act 1 (Setup) → Act 2 (Conflict) → Act 3 (Resolution)
- Emotional Connection Over Information - Lead with feeling, not features
- Show, Don't Tell - Let visuals reveal truth
- Authenticity as Competitive Advantage - Real imperfection beats polished artifice
- The Hero's Journey: Customer is hero, brand is mentor/tool
- Tension and Resolution - Without tension, there is no story
- Values Made Visible - Embody values in behavior, not just words
