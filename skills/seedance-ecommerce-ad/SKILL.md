## The 2-Second Hook Framework: E-Commerce Edition

12 proven hook patterns:

1. **Product Drop with Dramatic Impact** - Product descends with motion blur, dramatic lighting
2. **Satisfying Texture ASMR Close-Up** - Macro shot of product texture triggers sensory engagement
3. **Before/After Transformation** - Split-screen problem → solved state
4. **"Stop Scrolling" Direct Address** - Model looks at camera, hand gesture
5. **Unboxing Reveal** - Hands open premium packaging
6. **Color/Variant Cascade** - Products line up, rotating through variants in 2 seconds
7. **Ingredient Explosion** - Ingredients splinter outward from product center
8. **Problem-Solution Snap** - Text "Problem:" → cut → "Solution:" with product
9. **In-Hand Usage Tease** - Hands holding product, demonstrating key feature
10. **Lifestyle Aspiration Flash** - Quick cut to beautiful, relatable lifestyle scene
11. **Scarcity/Urgency Signal** - "Limited Drop" with product glow
12. **Creator Reaction** - Unboxing with genuine surprised expression

## E-Commerce Ad Philosophy: What Actually Sells

1. Hero Shots Are Non-Negotiable - Pure, isolated product shot with perfect lighting
2. Multi-Angle Storytelling - Rotate: front, back, side, detail
3. In-Context Usage - Product in real life, not sterile photography
4. Before/After Is Psychology Gold - Problem → realization → solution → happy outcome
5. Quick-Cut Energy - 0.5-1 second cuts; build momentum
6. Texture & Tactile Detail - Close-ups create tactile desire
7. Lifestyle Aspiration - Sell the life, not the product
8. Text Overlays That Convert - Problem identification, key benefit, social proof, pricing, CTA
