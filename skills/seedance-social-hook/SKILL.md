# The Art of the Viral Hook: Master Guide to Scroll-Stopping Social Media Videos

## The Science of the Hook: Why 2 Seconds Matter

**The Completion Rate Curve:**
- 0-2 seconds: Retention gate. 90%+ stay = algorithm promotes; 50%+ bounce = algorithm buries
- 2-5 seconds: "Hook confirmation" phase
- 5-15 seconds: Engagement zone
- 15+ seconds: Retention falls sharply

**5 Core Triggers:**

1. **Pattern Interrupt** - Brain violation: glitch, impossible speed, color explosion
2. **Curiosity Gap** - Missing information forces brain to keep watching
3. **Emotional Trigger** - Amygdala bypasses conscious scroll decision (cute/fear/awe/humor/inspiration)
4. **The Contrast Principle** - Silence→bang, stillness→movement, dull→flash
5. **Movement Detection** - Evolution wired us to notice movement in 200ms

## The Hook Encyclopedia: 25+ Proven Patterns

### Visual Shock Hooks
1. **Impossible Scale** - Something 50x normal size in first frame
2. **Impossible Physics** - Defying gravity; water flowing upward
3. **Color Explosion** - Grayscale world flooded with color at 1.5s
4. **Unexpected Reveal** - Camera positioned to hide full context until last frame
5. **Extreme Close-up Zoom** - Impossibly close macro, zoom out reveals subject
6. **Split Screen Transformation** - Left/right before/after changing live
7. **Freeze Frame Break** - Video appears frozen, then explodes into fast motion

### Curiosity Gap Hooks
8. **Half-Reveal** - Show 50-70% of something, force continuation
9. **Wait For It Setup** - "You won't believe what happens next"
10. **Before/After Tease** - Show shocking "after" first, cut to "before"
11. **Ladder Reveal** - Each cut reveals a little more (4 cuts = 4 rungs)
12. **Misdirection Hook** - Establish one expectation, deliver something different

### Emotional Trigger Hooks
13. **Cute Overload** - Extreme cuteness, soft lighting, warm colors
14. **Fear or Tension** - Unsettling visual, dangerous setup, ominous music
15. **Awe/Wonder** - Breathtaking: vast landscape, intricate detail, cosmic scale
16. **Humor/Absurdity** - Funny premise, perfectly timed punchline
17. **Inspiration/Aspiration** - Aspirational moment: athlete scoring, dream home reveal
18. **Shock/Disgust** - Jarring, gross, or shocking (use carefully)

### Pattern Interrupt Hooks
19. **Reverse Motion** - Action plays backward
20. **Glitch Effect** - Digital distortion (0.2-0.5 seconds)
21. **Negative Space Break** - Composition changes dramatically
22. **Audio Jump** - Sound completely different from visual expectation
23. **Rapid Cuts** - 4-8 cuts in first 2 seconds
24. **Focus Shift** - Depth of field changes rapidly

### Direct Address Hooks
25. **Camera Look** - Character makes eye contact, breaking fourth wall
26. **Direct Question** - "Wanna know why?" "Have you ever...?" 
27. **On-Screen Text Command** - "WAIT", "LOOK", "STOP SCROLLING"
